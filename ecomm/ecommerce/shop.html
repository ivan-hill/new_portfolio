Breaking, also known as breakdancing, b-boying, or b-girling could be the most common element of hip-hop.

Breaking is very unstructured and improvisational and is performed at all different levels: toprock (standing), downrock (close to the floor), power moves (acrobatics), and freeze moves (poses).

More important than the clothes you wear is the shoes you choose. You have to get your footwear right. Sneakers that give really good arch and ankle support are a must wear!
